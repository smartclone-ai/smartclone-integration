This document outlines the planned development roadmap for SmartClone Core. The roadmap is subject to change based on community feedback and priorities.

## Current Status: v0.1.0 (Initial Release)

The initial release provides the core foundation for hardware-adaptive AI scaling in browsers.

We believe in community-driven development. The roadmap priorities may shift based on:

- Community feedback and feature requests
- Bug reports and issue priorities
- Security concerns
- Industry trends and standards
- Partner and enterprise needs

## Version Support Policy

- **Current major version**: Full support with features and bug fixes
- **Previous major version**: Security fixes and critical bugs for 12 months
- **Older versions**: No official support (community support only)

---

## Release Schedule

- **Major versions** (x.0.0): Yearly
- **Minor versions** (0.x.0): Monthly
- **Patch versions** (0.0.x): As needed (bug fixes, security)
- **Pre-releases**: Available on `develop` branch
